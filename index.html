OUR HISTORY

Crave-Feast, founded in 2005 by Emily Bennett and Jake Thompson in Portland, quickly became renowned for its innovative, locally-sourced cuisine. The restaurant earned a Michelin star in 2012, boosting its national acclaim. Emphasizing sustainability and community, it introduced cooking classes and local partnerships. During the 2020 pandemic, Crave-Feast adapted to takeout and virtual offerings. Today, it remains a celebrated culinary destination.

INDIAN

Rich, authentic flavors with traditional spices. Signature dishes: Butter Chicken, Biryani, Paneer Tikka. Focus on both vegetarian and non-vegetarian specialties.

CHINESE

Balanced sweet, savory, and spicy flavors. Highlights: General Tso's Chicken, Peking Duck, Dim Sum. Fresh ingredients and traditional techniques.

ITALIAN

Rustic, heartwarming flavors with handmade pasta and wood-fired pizza. Notable dishes: Fettuccine Alfredo, Osso Buco, Risotto. Emphasis on authentic, home-style cooking.
